# distribution

Distribution-style samples.

## What belongs here
- boxplots
- violin plots
- histograms

## When to search this category first
- when the shape of a distribution is the main message
- when you have many raw observations per group
- when quartiles, density shape, or frequency counts matter more than a single mean value

## When to avoid this category
- when you only have one aggregated value per category
- when your data should be shown as a continuous profile over x
- when the figure is naturally a scatter or heatmap instead
